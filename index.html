ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm a Game Developer based in Spain with a strong foundation in programming and game design. Over the past years, I've worked on a variety of projects using both Unreal Engine and Unity, both for mobile and PC.

I consider myself a problem solver and a self-taught developer. I'm always looking for new challenges and opportunities to grow professionally and personally speaking. I take initiative to learn new technologies and tools to improve my skills and develop complete projects.

I'm currently working at Viva Games Studios, a mobile game publisher and developer, where I am responsible for the full game design and programming of After World, a zombie survival game soon to be released worldwide.

Feel free to contact me on any of my social media platforms or email me. I'll try to respond as soon as possible. Links are on the floating buttons on the bottom right of the page.
